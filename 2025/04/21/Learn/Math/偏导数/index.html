定义

设函数\(z=f(x, y)\)在点\((x_0, y_0)\)的某一邻域内有定义, 当\(y\)固定在\(y_0\)而\(x\)在\(x_0\)处有增量\(\Delta x\)时, 相应的函数有增量:

f(x0+Δx,y0)−f(x0,y0) f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0) f(x0​+Δx,y0​)−f(x0​,y0​)

若极限:

lim⁡Δx→0f(x0+Δx,y0)−f(x0,y0)Δx \lim\limits_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x} Δx→0lim​Δxf(x0​+Δx,y0​)−f(x0​,y0​)​

存在. 则称此极限为函数\(z=f(x, y)\)在点\((x_0, y_0)\)处对\(x\)的偏导数, 记作:

∂z∂x∣x=x0y=y0,∂f∂x∣x=x0y=y0, \left.\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right|_{\substack{x=x_0\\\\y=y_0}} , \left.\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\right|_{\substack{x=x_0\\\\y=y_0}} , ∂x∂z​∣∣∣∣∣​x=x0​y=y0​​​,∂x∂f​∣∣∣∣∣​x=x0​y=y0​​​,
